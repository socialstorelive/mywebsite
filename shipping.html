Shipping

At Social Store we want to make sure that you receive your shoes as quickly and conveniently as possible. Here are the details on our shipping options:

Shipping Options

We offer several shipping options to fit your needs and budget:

  • Standard Shipping: Free for all orders over $50.00, takes 5-7 business days.
  • Expedited Shipping: Available for an additional fee, takes 2-3 business days.
  • Overnight Shipping: Available for an additional fee, takes 1 business day.

Please note that these shipping times are estimates and may be affected by factors outside of our control, such as weather or carrier delays.


Order Processing

We strive to process and ship all orders as quickly as possible. Most orders are processed within 1-2 business days. If there are any delays or issues with your order, we will notify you by email or phone.


Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also log in to your account on our website to track your order status and view your tracking information.


Shipping Restrictions

Please note that we only ship to addresses within the United States. We are unable to ship to P.O. boxes, APO/FPO addresses, or international addresses at this time.
