Return Policy

At Social Store, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with your shoes, we offer a hassle-free return policy. Here are the details:

Returns

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. To be eligible for a return, your shoes must be in their original condition and packaging, with all tags and labels attached. Worn or damaged shoes will not be accepted for return.

Return Process

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team at [your customer service email or phone number]. We will provide you with a pre-paid shipping label to send your shoes back to us. Once we receive your return and verify that it meets our return policy requirements, we will issue a refund to your original payment method

within 5-7 business days.

Exchanges

If you would like to exchange your shoes for a different size or style, please contact our customer service team to initiate the exchange process. We will provide you with a pre-paid shipping label to send back the shoes you would like to exchange. Once we receive the shoes and verify that they meet our return policy requirements, we

will send out your replacement pair.

Non-Refundable Items

Please note that certain items are non-refundable, including gift cards, final sale items, and customized shoes. If you have any questions about whether an item is eligible for return, please contact our customer service team.
