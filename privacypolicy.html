Social Store - Privacy Policy

At Social Store, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers' personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose personal information in connection with our online shoe store.

Information we collect

We may collect the following types of personal information from you when you use our online shoe store:

  • Contact information, such as your name, email address, phone number, and shipping address
  • Payment information, such as your credit card number, expiration date, and security code
  • Purchase history and product preferences
  • Information you provide when you contact us or participate in surveys or promotions

We may collect this information directly from you, or from third-party sources such as payment processors.

Use of information

We use the personal information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders.
  • To communicate with you about your orders, account, and products and services.
  • To personalize your shopping experience and make product recommendations.
  • To improve our website and services, and to analyze trends and user behavior.
  • To prevent fraud and other illegal activities.
  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to legal requests.
